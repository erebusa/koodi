Office Assistant

Minimum of high school diploma as long as you have the following qualifications:

GENERAL REQUIREMENTS:
  • Must have above average in English communication skills (verbal and written), with sound decision making.
  • Highly organized and with proven ability to work in a fast-paced environment.
  • Must be meticulous in paper works & resourceful
  • Willing to work on extended hours
  • Trustworthy - Handles confidential matters and respects individual's right to privacy with tact, discretion and professional judgment.
  • Able to do repetitive and mundane administrative tasks
    •

TECHNICAL REQUIREMENTS:
  • CAN-DO attitude is required. You will be required to learn new terms and technologies all the time.
  • Must be proficient in using Microsoft Office applications particulary in Excel, Word and Powerpoint.
  • Must have basic knowledge about internet and web applications. You will need to learn new web technologies that we use on a daily basis.
  • Preferably with knowledge of relevant payroll, HR and staffing issues
    •

 

DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • Provide administrative service and support.
  • Receive, direct and relay telephone and fax messages
  • Performs keyboarding, messaging, copying, filing, and research.
  • Manage, update and schedule all internal and external meeting requests; calendar management, conference calls, set appointments, book travel arrangement and other work related transactions.
  • Prepares and creates presentations, proposals and reports.
  • Maintains adequate inventory levels of office equipment and supplies.
  • Provide administrative assistance and support to our clients and high-level officials during visits and meetings.
  • Bills payment and research
    •


Note:
PART TIME or FULL TIME onsite work in San Fernando City, Pampanga
